ABOUT avataran

Project Avataran is a project initiated and run by Enactus DTU, with the aim of rehabilitating acid attack survivors. We have devised a technique of reusing waste cooking oil to produce dishwashing liquid,we have trained our entrepreneurs in the production of the soap and have launched our product in retail. Our survivors run this project independently and we are expanding at an exponential rate, with an aim to place our product in larger retail chains and corporate distributions.

01.

CONCEPT

Project Avataran is an initiative that perfectly integrates entrepreneurial action with social and environmental cause. At its core is the rehabilitation of acid attack survivors by means of providing them a source of livelihood, and reintegrate them into society.

02.

DEVELOPMENT

Our team at Enactus DTU collaborated with the internationally acclaimed NGO ‘Make Love Not Scars’. We conceptualised a business model that places the acid attack survivors as the primary beneficiary and sole shareholders of the Project. The core operations of the project are : Skill Training, Production,Marketing and Sales.

03.

EXPANSION

We are currently looking towards expanding our market base by collaborating with larger corporations ,with a long term goal of supplying to the entire NCR region. We also plan to bring more survivors under the project and diversify our products.
